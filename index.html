About Roni

A little about me

Hi, I'm Roni — an artist with a deep love for both realism and imagination. My work spans charcoal portraiture, acrylic painting, and surrealist composition, always looking for the story hidden inside a face, a landscape, or a dream.

Whether it's the texture of graphite on paper or the boldness of colour on canvas — every piece I create is a conversation between the subject and the soul. Welcome to my world.
